What is Circular Economy?

The circular economy is a system that designs out waste and pollution, keeps products and materials in use, and regenerates natural systems.

Reduce

Minimise waste by rethinking design and consumption.

Reuse

Design products for repair and second life.

Recycle

Recover materials and close the loop.

Start small — start local

Even a school project can become a model for the community. Document local entrepreneurs, run a repair café, or create upcycled products.
