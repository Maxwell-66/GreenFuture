About Circular Economy & Green Entrepreneurship

The circular economy is an alternative to the traditional linear economy (make → use → dispose). It focuses on designing products and systems so materials are reused, refurbished, and recycled.

Why it matters for students

  • Learn real-world problem solving
  • Create local impact
  • Build entrepreneurial skills
