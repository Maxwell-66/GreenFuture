Student Project Ideas

Upcycle Crafts Studio

Collect old clothes and plastics, create bags, wallets and school supplies. Sell at school fairs.

Community Compost Hub

Run a collection for organic waste and turn it into compost to sell to gardeners.

Repair Café

Organise a weekend event to fix small appliances and clothes — teach repair skills and reduce waste.

Solar Charger Kits

Assemble small solar lamps/kits and sell them to households without reliable electricity.

Tip: Document local costs and suppliers — judges like practical planning and local sourcing.
